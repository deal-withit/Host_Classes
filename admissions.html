ADMISSION

For BCA

Students of BCA who wants to learn programing, will have to take admission in month of march each year. If anyone want to learn only languages without having any degree then they are advised to join the batch which includes your desired language. The admission time of host classes is according to the university timing of admission. However most of the new batches are started from month of May. The process of admission is very simple . Just pay a visit to our institute ...

Many batches run in host throughout the day. You can choose any batch according to your time management.

Take Care of these few points before taking an admission.

  • Visit personally to the institution for admission.

  • Limited seats are availiable. Admission is done on the basis of first come first serve process.

  • Any type of payment is not required at the time of admission.

  • If you are confused about admission or the stream then you are advised to meet Raj Sir

  • There is no any outer source of taking admission in host classes so be aware of frauds.
